About Me

Growing up, I always knew that I would end up doing some kind of programming or coding. Originally I wanted to be a video game programmer, then as I got older I started having other interests and put my passion on hold, as I explored being young and stupid. Coming from a Deaf household, I wanted to try out interpreting and working with Deaf students, and many of my students are who encouraged me to continue pursuing my interests in computer sciences.

Now, I am a skilled web developer, and can write code to create my own website. I am hard working, and continue to balance my time management with everything else in my life. I know my strengths; time-management, fast learner, and avid teammate; and my weaknesses; I can be too direct. Everyday I continue to develop and learn as I strengthen my strengths and my weaknesses.
